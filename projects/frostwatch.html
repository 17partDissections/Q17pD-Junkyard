Frostwatch

Project type: Computer game
First mention: 18.12.2025
Last update: 14.02.2026
Status: In development
Github repo: 17partDissections/Frostwatch

Platform: Windows, Linux
Programming Language: C#
Engine: Unity 3D
Localization: English, Russian





Frostwatch is an atmospheric(or not. atleast i tried:sob:) point-and-click survival horror game set in a winter wilderness. You are stranded with limited resources and monsters lurking in the woods, and the only resistance you can offer them - is an old and slow Mosin Nagant.

Keep your rifle nearby!

Monsters are not waiting. Defend your life with your only Mosin Nagant.

Keep your campfire alive, unless...

Your campfire is your only source of heat and light. Let it die - and the cold or something worse will come for your life.

Dont forget to eat!

Oh, it's the easiest one. You've got a lot of supplies so don't worry about that. Wait, what do you mean you have only 2 food cans left?

And one more thing...

The only way to quit the Game scene - is to shoot yourself IN-GAME(Cuz why not).
