Week at School Toilet - is a horror point-and-click game made by MBW studio. I mainly worked on programming.

Game set throughout the third floor of School No. ###
The antagonists are:
— Gladkova
— Launec
— Nemec
— Harchev
— FatGuy
— ####

The player finds themselves in a school restroom at night and, with access to security cameras, monitors the teachers movements.
Meanwhile, they patrol the floor, trying to find the player. The player can turn on a flashlight to check if a teacher is nearby, close doors, and view security cameras.
All these actions consume energy, so it's important to conserve it.
