Alesha558CardBot

Project type: Telegram bot
First mention: 03.10.2025
Release date: 29.10.2025
Last update: 06.12.2025
Status: Cancelled
Github repo: None

Platform: Telegram
Programming Language: Python
Localization: Russian











Alesha558CardBot is my second Telegram bot.

I continued the tradition of making funny bots with my friends in there. This time I made a loot-box simulator. Every 24 hours, users could try their luck and get a random card featuring Alesha — each card had its own rarity and condition.
In total, there were these rarities: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic,and Legendary.
The condition level just added some scratches to the card so it looked worn out.
Users could save their dropped cards in their card library. They could also check out other people's cards.

I turned this bot off for the same reason as my first bot. The person pictured on every card wasn't exactly happy about it.
