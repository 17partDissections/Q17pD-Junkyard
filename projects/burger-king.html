So, one evening I got really bored and decided to spend a couple of hours making some eyesore of a game with about 5 minutes of gameplay:
Introducing — Burger King!
(Yeah, I don't have much creativity. Or a vocabulary bigger than 500 words.)

The concept is simple:
A fat geography teacher heard from across the school that someone disrespected his favorite fast-food chain!
Make your way through a school hallway crawling with students and kick that bastard's ass.
...And you know what? That's not so easy to pull off.
